Projects

LoanItToMe

I created LoanItToMe, a Ruby on Rails website with a Backbone.js front-end. The idea behind this site is to support a share economy. It seems wasteful for everyone in a neighborhood to own a lawn mower they only need to use once a week. Perhaps we could just share from each other, but of course this requires a lot of trust. If the neighbor's kids runs leaves it out in the rain, and rusts over... Sharing is hard. The website is currently just a mock-up with sample data and a Google Map. If you have any questions or comments, go ahead an email me.
